About

I'm Daniel Dale Igwebuike — developer, writer, and Diocesan Choirmaster of the St. Cecilia Choir Warri Diocese. I go by DaleX.

I build full-stack web applications with a bias toward things that are genuinely useful in the real world — not demos, not templates. Production systems that organisations and communities actually run on.

My work sits at an unusual intersection: institutional technology for Catholic organisations, Web3 tools for on-chain users, and content strategy for emerging blockchain protocols. Most developers pick one lane. I've found that crossing them produces better thinking in all three.

I'm also the Delta State Head of ICT for the MATAN Food Bank Foundation, and Planning Committee Chairman for the SCCWD — which means I build systems and then sit in the room where they're used.

Featured Build — LMS Platform
Catholic Music Academy LMS

A full Learning Management System commissioned by the Catholic Diocese of Warri. Handles the complete student lifecycle: online application with payment receipt upload, admission review, sequential module delivery, MCQ and written assessments, lecturer grading, exam scheduling, and digital certification. Built to production standard with multi-role access, PBKDF2 security, and a zero-dependency vanilla stack.

Multi-role admin panel — Master Admin and Lecturer with scoped permissions
Assessment engine supporting MCQ, written responses, randomised question banks, and per-module pass marks
Complete email pipeline via Resend — application, admission, grading, completion, examination
YouTube facade + invisible IFrame audio player for in-module media
Vanilla JS Supabase Vercel Resend PBKDF2 PWA-ready
